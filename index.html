What is it?

Test of a machine's ability to exhibit intelligent behavior equivalent to, or indistinguishable from, that of a human.

How it works

-There are three terminals.

-Two of the terminals are operated by humans, and the third terminal is operated by a computer.

-One human is designated as the questioner.

-The other human and the computer are designated the respondents.

Alan Turing

-Invented the turing test in 1951 and was originally called "The Imitation Game

-Involved a male and a female and the judge had to guess who was who
